proposal

For this project I want to create a site centered around the cabins that have been in my family for a very long time. The cabins are old and filled with so much memorabilia, in a way they are a time capsule themselves. As for translating this into a project, I am thinking of making something centered around my memories of this place as a child and my deeper connection to it. I am currently trying to figure out the exact form but this could take the form of an interactive map or a scrollable interface to take the viewer through different settings within. So lots of clicking on different assets but this is something that I am still considering. I am also playing with the idea of having a place where the viewer can reflect and leave their own thoughts but I am not fully decided on that quite yet.

As for the lenses, I think part of this project may focus on the ideas of modernization vs keeping the age of places. In a way questioning why it is necessary to modernize but also what will these cabins look like in the future as I get older. I definitely think some parts need to be updated and repaired as time goes on, but how do you keep the charm of what makes this place unique. It also could be a way to capture the cabins in the state that they are in now. I am finding that I am still working out the central focus of this project and still have lots of ideas right now.

For this project, I would say that my audience is likely for a future version of myself. I am someone who cares very deeply about my interactions with loved ones, memories of certain places, how places change and nature. I have very fond memories of this place but as I have gotten older, I see a lot of the challenges of upkeeping a place like this. I may think differently in 10 years as someone who is in their late 20’s/early 30’s compared to now being in my early to 20’s. I think this project could also be aimed at those I am close with in my family. I would say I am definitely exploring if I want to open up my target audience a bit, but I do think by creating something personally focused, outside viewers will still gain a strong interest.
