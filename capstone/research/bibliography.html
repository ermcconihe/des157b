Jansen-Olliges, Sheri, From Timber Barons to Tree Huggers: The Story of Middleton's Redwood Community, CreateSpace Independent Publishing Platform, 2012.
This book cover much of the history of the family who created Middleton Tract, the history of the area and does a small deepdive into the families who were a part of the creation of this place and who are still around. My family is mentioned quite a bit in here and it gives insight into our larger relations to the tract as a whole.

“Middleton Tract, California”, Wikipedia, 2025, https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Middleton_Tract,_California
This is a shortened overview of the history of Middleton Tract. It mostly covers the location of the area and a short history of its founding along with a case in the 80's covering logging rights and preservation.

Another one of my sources will be information from my Aunt that I am currently gathering. She is very knowledgeable about the history of the place and has kept track of much of the family history.
